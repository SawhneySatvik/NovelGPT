Applications



Medical assistant


Artificial intelligence is poised to broadly reshape medicine, potentially improving the experiences of both clinicians and patients. We realized the potential of AI in medicine, to make healthcare more accurate, efficient and accessible for patients worldwide. Thus, we have taken a step forward in the same direction, trying to help both professionals as well as patients.

NovelGPT is an AI-based medical assistant that assists medical professionals in patient care, diagnosis, and treatment planning. It provides with a detailed explanation of the patient's diseases and requirements, and gives the desired solutions for the same.

The program is designed to provide real-time data analysis and decision-making support, ensuring personalized patient care and improved clinical outcomes. It Ensures data privacy and security for both patients and medical professionals. It Develops personalized treatment plans based on patient data, medical history, and other relevant information.

We have built the program using python. We used multiple libraries in python such as pytorch and tensorflow. We have supplied the program with tons of medical data to analyse and provide the required solutions for various diseases.
